Onyx

Onyx had to get 3 inches of his tail amputated due to an injury.

  • Age: 7 years
  • Food: Anything edible
  • Breed: Beagle/Chow Mix
  • Color: Black

Onyx is a beagle/chow mix dog that we rescued from a beagle rescue organization. They didn't believe he was a beagle at first because of his color. They ended up doing a DNA test that proved he's 70% beagle, 27% chow and 3% poodle.
